This Privacy Policy explains what information AuraAI ("we," "us," "our") collects when you use the AuraAI Discord bot and Dashboard (the "Service"), how we use it, and the choices you have. This policy is specific to AuraAI — it is separate from the privacy policy covering ShelfSmart.

1. Information We Collect

Information from Discord

  • Your Discord user ID, username, and avatar (via Discord's API and OAuth, when you log into the Dashboard);
  • Server (guild) ID, channel IDs, and role IDs that a server owner configures for AuraAI's features;
  • Message content you send directly to AuraAI's commands (e.g., /chat, /ask-td), which is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response;
  • BattleTags, usernames, or other in-game identifiers you provide for stat lookups.

Tournament, Match & Coaching Data

Team rosters, match results, hero/map bans, tournament registrations, coaching session transcripts (logged when a coaching thread ends), and scrim schedules you or a server admin submit through AuraAI's commands or Dashboard.

Payment Information

If you purchase Pro Monthly or Lifetime access, payment is processed directly by Stripe. We do not receive or store your full card number; we retain only what Stripe provides us to confirm your subscription status (e.g., subscription ID, tier, payment status).

Social Alert Configuration

If a server admin sets up Twitch, YouTube, or Kick alerts, we store the connected channel/creator identifiers needed to check for new streams or uploads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate core bot features: tournaments, matches, coaching, stats, AI chat, temporary voice channels, and social alerts;
  • To authenticate you and load your server's configuration in the Dashboard;
  • To process and confirm subscription payments via Stripe;
  • To respond to support tickets submitted through the Dashboard;
  • To maintain and improve the Service, including diagnosing bugs.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain server configuration, tournament, and match data for as long as AuraAI remains active in a server, so that history (past tournaments, match logs) stays available. Coaching transcripts are retained to allow dispute review. You or a server admin can request deletion of specific data by submitting a support ticket through the Dashboard; see Section 6.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13, consistent with Discord's own minimum age requirement. We do not knowingly collect information from users we know to be under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, contact us via a support ticket and we will remove it.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access or deletion: submit a support ticket through the AuraAI Dashboard to request a copy of, or deletion of, your data.
  • Removing the bot: a server admin can remove AuraAI from a server at any time via Discord's server settings; this stops further data collection from that server going forward.
  • Dashboard login: you can revoke AuraAI's OAuth access at any time from your Discord Authorized Apps settings.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted database connections, restricted service-role access) to protect stored data, but no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to or stored by the Service.
